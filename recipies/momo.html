momo

Description

Momo is a dumpling that is made from wheat flour which is used to cover the fillings inside the dumpling.The fillings mostly include meat,tofu and vegetables.

They are famous in countries like Tibet,Nepal,China and India. They are very cheap and fast to make/get so it is mainly consumed at lunch.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Grinded Meat (could be chicken or buff)
  • Cabbages
  • Onions,Ginger
  • Vegetable oil
  • Tumeric powder,coriander powder

If you are vegetarian you could replace the meat with tofu or paneer.

Steps

  1. Take 750 gm of flour in a bowl and add 2 cups of water and knead it
  2. Let the flour rest until we prepare our fillings
  3. Chop Onions,Cabbages into small pieces
  4. Take 1 kg of Grounded meat into a large bowl
  5. Add the chopped Cabbages,Onions,Tumeric powder,coriander powder and salt(accorind to taste)
  6. Roll the dough with a roller and cut the rolled dough with the head of the cup so you get even circular rolled dough
    7. Add fillings to the circular dough and make dumplings
  7. Add about 10 to 20 pcs in the steamer and leave it for 30 min
  8. Your momo are ready
