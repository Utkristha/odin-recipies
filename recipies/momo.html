momo

Description

Momo is a dumpling that is made from wheat flour which is used to cover the fillings inside the dumpling.The fillings mostly include meat,tofu and vegetables.

They are famous in countries like Tibet,Nepal,China and India. They are very cheap and fast to make/get so it is mainly consumed at lunch.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Grinded Meat (could be chicken or buff)
  • Cabbages

If you are vegetarian you could replace the meat with tofu or paneer.
