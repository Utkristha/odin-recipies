Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is a simple dish made by adding cheese to any type of bread and melting it. It is a tasty food which can be made easily.

Ingredients

  • Any bread you want.
  • Mozzarella and vintage cheddar cheese
  • Salted butter

Steps

  1. Grate the cheese with a grater
  2. Butter both side of bread (all 4 side)
  3. Place the bread into the pan and heat it slowly for a min
  4. Flip the bread and add the cheese and place the other piece of bread on top
  5. Grill it for 3 min and the turn the sandwich and grill other side for 3 min
  6. take it out of the pan and cut it and enjoy your sandwich
