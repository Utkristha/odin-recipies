Steak

Description

Steak is a cut of meat from cattle.They are mostly meat without any bone.

Ingredients

  • Cut of meat for the steak
  • Butter
  • salt and pepper
  • oil
  • garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Use paper towel to dry pat the steak
  2. Sprinkle each side with salt and pepper according to your liking
  3. Pre heat a cooking pan and brush it with oil
  4. Add steak and shear it until brown crust appears
  5. add butter and garlic cloves, tilt the pan on one side to spoon the butter over the steak
  6. remove the steak and let it rest for 10 min
